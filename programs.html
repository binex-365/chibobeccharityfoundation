Our Programs

Impacting lives through sustainable, timely, and community-driven initiatives.

Program Calendar

Back-to-School Drive

August - September (Annually)

Distributing school supplies, uniforms, and scholarships to children in need before the new academic year begins.

Monthly Food Outreach

Last Saturday of Every Month

Providing nutritious food packs and groceries to vulnerable families in various communities.

Health & Wellness Camps

Quarterly

Free medical checkups, health education, and distribution of hygiene kits to children and adults.

Women & Youth Empowerment

March & October

Skill acquisition workshops, entrepreneurship training, and micro-grants for women and young people.

Emergency Relief Response

As Needed

Rapid response to disasters and crises with food, shelter, and psychosocial support.

More About Our Programs

Community Engagement

We work closely with local leaders, schools, and health centers to ensure our programs are tailored to the unique needs of each community. Our team conducts regular needs assessments and feedback sessions to improve our impact.

Volunteer Opportunities

Volunteers are the heart of Chibobec. From logistics and outreach to mentorship and training, we offer meaningful roles for individuals and groups who want to make a difference.

Monitoring & Evaluation

Every program is tracked and evaluated for effectiveness. We use data and real stories to measure our success and share transparent reports with our supporters and partners.

Partnerships

We collaborate with NGOs, government agencies, and private organizations to expand our reach and resources. If your organization shares our vision, partner with us.

How You Can Help

  • Become a monthly donor to sustain our ongoing projects.
  • Volunteer your time or professional skills.
  • Organize a fundraiser or awareness event in your community.
  • Share our mission on social media to inspire others.
