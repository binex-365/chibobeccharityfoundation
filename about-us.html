About Chibobec

Empowering families. Transforming lives. Building a brighter future for every child.

Who We Are

Chibobec Charity Foundation is a registered non-profit organization founded in 2025 with the vision to create lasting change in the lives of vulnerable children, women, and families in Nigeria and beyond. We are driven by compassion, integrity, and a deep commitment to social justice.

Health Outreach

Our foundation was born out of the desire to bridge the gap between the privileged and the less privileged, ensuring that every child and family has access to basic needs, quality education, healthcare, and opportunities for a better life.

Our Purposes

  • Alleviate Poverty: We provide food, clothing, and essential supplies to families in need, especially in rural and underserved communities.
  • Promote Education: We offer scholarships, school supplies, mentorship, and after-school programs to empower children and youth to reach their full potential.
  • Improve Health: We organize free medical outreaches, health education seminars, and provide access to clean water and sanitation facilities.
  • Empower Women & Youth: We run skill acquisition, entrepreneurship, and empowerment programs to help women and young people become self-reliant.
  • Foster Community Spirit: We encourage volunteerism, unity, and social responsibility through community projects and events.
  • Disaster Relief: We respond to emergencies and disasters by providing relief materials and psychosocial support to affected families.

Our Story

Chibobec Charity Foundation started as a small group of friends volunteering in local communities. Over the years, our passion grew into a movement, and today, we have impacted thousands of lives through our various programs and partnerships. Our founder, Chibuike Chukwunyere Timothy, was inspired by his own childhood experiences and the desire to give back to society.

How We Work

  • We identify the most pressing needs in communities through research and engagement.
  • We collaborate with local leaders, schools, health centers, and other NGOs for greater impact.
  • We mobilize resources from donors, partners, and volunteers to implement our projects.
  • We monitor and evaluate our programs to ensure transparency, accountability, and sustainability.

Our Impact

  • Over 5,000 children supported with education and nutrition.
  • More than 1,200 families empowered through food drives and economic support.
  • 300+ volunteers engaged in various outreach programs.
  • 20+ communities reached across different states.

Our Vision

A world where every child and family has access to the resources and opportunities they need to succeed, regardless of their background or circumstances.

Our Mission

To empower families and children through education, nutrition, healthcare, and community support programs, fostering hope, dignity, and self-reliance.

Our Core Values

  • Compassion – We serve with empathy and kindness.
  • Integrity – We are transparent and accountable.
  • Collaboration – We believe in the power of working together.
  • Empowerment – We help families and children build brighter futures.
  • Respect – We honor the dignity and potential of every person.
  • Innovation – We seek creative solutions to social challenges.

Our Goals

  • To provide nutritious meals and groceries to at least 1,000 families annually.
  • To support the education of 2,000 children each year through scholarships, materials, and mentorship.
  • To organize monthly outreach programs and health fairs for underserved communities.
  • To empower 500 women and youth annually through skill acquisition and entrepreneurship training.
  • To grow our volunteer network to 1,000 active members by 2026.
  • To ensure transparency, accountability, and measurable impact in all our activities.

Frequently Asked Questions

  • How can I volunteer?
    Visit our Contact page or email us to join our volunteer network. We welcome people of all backgrounds!
  • How are donations used?
    Donations go directly to our programs, including food drives, scholarships, health outreaches, and empowerment projects. We provide regular updates and reports to our donors.
  • Can I partner with Chibobec?
    Yes! We welcome partnerships with individuals, organizations, and companies. Contact us to discuss how we can work together.
  • Where do you operate?
    We are based in Nigeria but serve communities across several states and are expanding our reach every year.
  • How can I get help?
    If you or someone you know needs support, please reach out through our contact page or visit our office during our monthly outreach events.
