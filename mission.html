Our Mission

Empowering families. Transforming lives. Building a brighter future for every child.

Our Mission Statement

At Chibobec Charity Foundation, our mission is to empower vulnerable children, women, and families by providing access to education, healthcare, nutrition, and economic opportunities. We are committed to fostering hope, dignity, and self-reliance in every community we serve.

What Drives Us

  • Compassion: Every person deserves dignity, care, and support.
  • Equity: We bridge the gap between privilege and need, ensuring opportunity for all.
  • Empowerment: We focus on sustainable solutions that help people become self-reliant.
  • Community: We build strong partnerships and encourage volunteerism for lasting impact.
  • Innovation: We embrace new ideas and technology to solve social challenges.
  • Integrity: We are transparent, accountable, and trustworthy in all our actions.

Why Our Mission Matters

In Nigeria and across Africa, millions of children and families face daily struggles with poverty, hunger, lack of education, and limited access to healthcare. Our mission is to break this cycle by providing practical support and long-term empowerment. We believe that every child deserves a chance to dream, every woman deserves a voice, and every family deserves hope.

  • We reach out to remote and underserved communities, ensuring no one is left behind.
  • We focus on both immediate relief (food, health, shelter) and sustainable change (education, skills, advocacy).
  • We measure our success by the smiles we create, the lives we uplift, and the communities we transform.

Our Goals

  • 1. Alleviate Poverty:
    • Provide nutritious meals and groceries to at least 1,000 families annually.
    • Distribute clothing, hygiene kits, and essential supplies to those in need.
    • Offer emergency relief during disasters and crises.
  • 2. Promote Education:
    • Support the education of 2,000 children each year through scholarships, school supplies, and mentorship.
    • Organize after-school programs, literacy classes, and digital skills workshops.
    • Partner with schools to improve learning environments and teacher training.
  • 3. Improve Health:
    • Organize free medical outreaches, health screenings, and vaccination drives.
    • Provide access to clean water and sanitation facilities in rural communities.
    • Promote health education on nutrition, hygiene, and disease prevention.
  • 4. Empower Women & Youth:
    • Run skill acquisition and entrepreneurship programs for women and young people.
    • Offer micro-grants and business mentorship to help start small businesses.
    • Promote gender equality and leadership opportunities for girls and women.
  • 5. Foster Community Spirit:
    • Encourage volunteerism and civic engagement through community projects.
    • Organize events that promote unity, peace, and social responsibility.
    • Work with local leaders and organizations for greater impact.
  • 6. Disaster Relief & Advocacy:
    • Respond rapidly to emergencies with food, shelter, and psychosocial support.
    • Advocate for the rights of children, women, and marginalized groups.
    • Engage with government and stakeholders for policy change and social justice.

Our Impact So Far

  • Over 5,000 children supported with education and nutrition.
  • More than 1,200 families empowered through food drives and economic support.
  • 300+ volunteers engaged in various outreach programs.
  • 20+ communities reached across different states.
  • Countless lives changed through hope, love, and opportunity.

Every month, we organize outreach programs, health fairs, and empowerment workshops to reach more people and create lasting change.

Stories of Change

Mary's Story: Once unable to attend school, Mary now excels in her studies thanks to our scholarship and mentorship program.
Chinedu's Family: After losing their home to a flood, Chinedu's family received food, shelter, and hope through our rapid response team.
Women of Hope: Over 100 women have started small businesses after completing our skills training, lifting their families out of poverty.

Our Core Values

  • Compassion – We serve with empathy and kindness.
  • Integrity – We are transparent and accountable in all our actions.
  • Collaboration – We believe in the power of working together.
  • Empowerment – We help families and children build brighter futures.
  • Respect – We honor the dignity and potential of every person.
  • Innovation – We seek creative solutions to social challenges.
