Our Mission

Chibobec Charity Foundation is a non-profit organization committed to uplifting underprivileged families and children within our communities. Founded on the belief that every child deserves the opportunity to thrive, we focus on providing essential support to parents who face challenges in meeting their children’s basic needs.

Our Programs

Food Assistance

We provide nutritious meals and groceries to families in need, ensuring no child goes to bed hungry.

Education Support

We supply school materials, scholarships, and mentorship to help children achieve their academic dreams.

Healthcare Access

We connect families with essential healthcare services and provide health education for a better future.
